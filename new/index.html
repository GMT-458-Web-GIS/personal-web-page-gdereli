What is geomatics engineering ?

Geomatics Engineering is a branch of engineering focused on collecting, processing, analyzing, and deriving meaningful insights from spatial data related to the Earth. Technologies such as mapping, Geographic Information Systems (GIS), remote sensing, and GPS/GNSS are used to measure location, area, and elevation, which are then applied to various projects. This discipline plays a crucial role in fields such as engineering, construction, environment, urban planning, and agriculture. Its primary goal is to provide accurate and up-to-date geographic data to support the planning and management processes of projects.
